BETOPIA PRODUCTS
================================================================
Betopia builds its own proprietary software products. These are ready-to-deploy platforms businesses can use directly or that partners can resell under white-label arrangements.

Product 1: Betopia HRM Software
----------------------------------------------------------------
A smart HR management system for modern organizations.
Key features:
- Employee database and profile management
- Payroll processing and tax calculation
- Attendance and leave management
- Performance reviews and goal tracking
- Recruitment and hiring workflow management
- Training and career development programs
Best for: Any business that wants to digitize and automate their HR operations.

Product 2: Betopia ERP
----------------------------------------------------------------
A fully integrated enterprise resource planning platform built on the Odoo framework.
Key features:
- Real-time data synchronization across all business modules
- Customizable workflows and business reports
- Mobile-friendly interface for access from anywhere
- Industry-leading security and compliance built in
Best for: Businesses that want a single system to manage finance, inventory, operations, and supply chain.

Product 3: Betopia Count Trust (Voting System)
----------------------------------------------------------------
A secure, enterprise-grade digital voting platform for organizations conducting elections or internal votes.
Key features:
- Smart registration and voter verification
- Role-based dashboards for administrators and voters
- Secure transport voting
- Integrated payment and membership management
Best for: Corporations, associations, government bodies, and universities conducting elections or governance votes.

Product 4: Betopia Agentic AI
----------------------------------------------------------------
An AI-powered agent that automates sales and customer engagement.
Best for: Businesses that want to automate sales outreach, customer follow-up, and engagement workflows without additional headcount.

Product 5: Betopia Talkora AI
----------------------------------------------------------------
An AI system that handles, answers, understands, and manages business calls 24/7.
Best for: Businesses that want intelligent call handling so they never miss a customer inquiry, even outside business hours.

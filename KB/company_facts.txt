Who We Are
----------------------------------------------------------------
Betopia Limited is a global enterprise technology company founded in Bangladesh. It competes with industry giants like Infosys and TCS, serving approximately 300 enterprise clients across healthcare, banking, manufacturing, and technology sectors.

Betopia is self-funded, which means decisions are made in the client's best interest without outside investor pressure.

Certifications: ISO 27001, SOC 2, CMMI
Technology Partners: Microsoft Gold, AWS Advanced, Google Cloud, Odoo, Salesforce, Fortinet, Trend Micro
Betopia has created 3,500+ jobs globally across IT, fintech, cloud, education, and supply chain sectors.

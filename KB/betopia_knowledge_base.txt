Betopia offers structured partnership programs for IT firms, consultants, and independent sales professionals who want to sell or resell Betopia's solutions and products.
Each partner is assigned a dedicated Partner Success Manager (PSM) from day one.

Partnership Model Overview
----------------------------------------------------------------
Global Engineering Hub: 5,000+ developers in Bangladesh providing 24/7 delivery
Partner Role: Own client relationships and commercial introductions
Betopia Role: Own delivery, technical risk, and warranty liability
Commission Protection: 12-month exclusive rights via "Deal Lock" in Partner Portal

----------------------------------------------------------------
Stream A - Custom Software & Projects (One-Time)
Partner commission on project sales
Includes warranty
Example: Custom AI apps, cloud migrations, cybersecurity builds

Stream B - Managed Services/AMC (Recurring)
Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC): A certain percentage of original license value annually
Monthly SLA/retainer agreements
Predictable "mailbox money"

Stream C - Resource Augmentation (Staffing)
Junior Developer
Mid-Level Developer
Senior Developer
QA/Automation
UI/UX Designer
DevOps/Cloud Engineer

Tech Lead/Architect

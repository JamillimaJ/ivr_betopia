SECTION 5: PARTNERSHIP PROGRAMS
================================================================

Betopia offers structured partnership programs for IT firms, consultants, and independent sales professionals who want to sell or resell Betopia's solutions and products.

Each partner is assigned a dedicated Partner Success Manager from day one.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Partnership Type 1: Sales Partner (Co-Branded)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Sell Betopia solutions under the Betopia brand alongside your own.

Revenue share: 30% of project value
Annual partnership fee: $1,500 to $2,500 per year
Includes: Sales training, marketing materials, dedicated Partner Success Manager, sales enablement support

----------------------------------------------------------------
Partnership Type 2: White-Label Partner
----------------------------------------------------------------

Deliver Betopia solutions entirely under your own brand. Clients see your company, not Betopia.

Revenue share: 40% of project value
Annual partnership fee: $3,500 to $5,000 per year
Includes: Full project delivery by Betopia, dedicated Partner Success Manager, co-marketing support, access to all Betopia products for resale

Products available for white-label resale: Cloud solutions, Cybersecurity, ERP, HRM Software, Count Trust Voting System, AI solutions, Digital Transformation services.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Partnership Type 3: Global Sales Agent Program
----------------------------------------------------------------

For independent sales professionals who want to earn commission by selling Betopia solutions without managing delivery.

Commission: Flat 15% on every successful deal with no earning cap.
No annual partnership fee for this program.

What the sales agent does:
- Identifies and engages potential clients in their region
- Understands client needs and introduces relevant Betopia solutions
- Arranges meetings and supports the sales closing process
- Represents Betopia professionally in all engagements

What Betopia handles:
- Solution guidance and pre-sales assistance
- Technical expertise during client meetings
- Proposals, pricing, and solution architecture
- Contracts, invoicing, and payment collection
- Project delivery and post-sales client support

Tools provided to sales agents: Company profile, corporate brochures, sales presentations, case studies, proposal and pricing support, official sales agent credentials.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Who Can Become a Partner
----------------------------------------------------------------

- IT and consulting firms looking to expand their service offerings
- Independent sales agents and technology consultants
- Companies seeking a trusted technology delivery partner for enterprise clients
- Solo consultants are welcome as long as they meet engagement expectations

----------------------------------------------------------------
How the Partnership Process Works
----------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Apply and get verified as a partner.
Step 2: Complete training on Betopia's offerings including Cloud, AI, Cybersecurity, ERP, and proprietary products.
